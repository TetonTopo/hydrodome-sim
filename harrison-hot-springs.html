About

▼

HydroDome uses Nelson Big Gun 100 Series sprinklers mounted on 30ft towers to create humidity domes that protect structures from wildfire. Each tower covers ~0.50 ha (80m diameter) at 200 GPM, 70 PSI. Towers operate on duty cycling via Capstone C65 microturbine-powered zones. V10 adds a 4-level hydraulic cascade refill model -- each tower gets priority-based refill constrained by its cistern connection (750 GPM), street pipe capacity, trunk line, and the F1 reservoir feed bottleneck. Emptiest cisterns get flow first. Water network data sourced from OpenStreetMap.
