About

▼

HydroDome uses industrial-grade Big Gun sprinklers mounted on 30ft towers to create humidity domes that protect structures from wildfire. Each tower covers ~7 ha (150m radius) at 720–850 GPM. River-draw pump system supplies water directly from Fraser/Quesnel Rivers. This model simulates the West Fraser Quesnel Sawmill complex for pilot deployment feasibility.
